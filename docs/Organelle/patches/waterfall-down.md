A simple polyphonic synthesizer that plays back notes in decreasing octaves.

## Details

Play notes back in decreasing octaves with an adjustable range and rate in one of four voices.

Knob1: Tuning

Knob2: Octave Range

Knob3: Roll Speed

Knob4: Decay Time

Aux and Foot Switch: Waveform
