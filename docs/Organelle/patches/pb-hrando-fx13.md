Polyrhythmic sample player with sample randomization and thirteen built-in effects. If you listen really hard, your settings *will* loop, we swear.

## Details

Three-page patch combining the Polybeats sequencer with the HRando sample player and feeding it into an FX. Since this is a multipage patch each knob will have different roles depending on page.

The Polybeats page sets how often a trigger is set (a 'Division' of 4 will trigger every quarter note. A Division of 8 will trigger every half note, and so on) to play a sample. The HRando component of this patch is responsible for playing the samples. When HRando receives each Polybeat sequencer trigger, HRando randomly plays one of as many as four samples stored on that key.

Load your own samples into this patch. In the patch folder, there are 24 folders in the HRando folder. The folder name numbers correspond to the Organelle key number. Place 1-4 of your samples in each folder!

Aux stops/starts/clears the polyrhythm.

Link enabled for syncing with other devices on a shared wireless network.
