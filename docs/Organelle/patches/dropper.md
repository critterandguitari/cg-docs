Tone synth with frequency drop.

## Details

Play notes and then hit the aux button to drop the frequency to 0.

Knob1: Tune

Knob2: Drop Time (10-1010ms)

Knob3: Tone

Knob4: Decay

Aux and Foot Switch: Drop the freq.
