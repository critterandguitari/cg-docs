# Recorder


**Tags:** Recorder, Sampler

**[Download Patch](http://thepeacetreaty.org/organelle/patches/Recorder.zip)**

Simple audio recorder.

## Details

Press the Aux button to begin a new recording, and again to end the recording. The length and number of recordings are limited only by the size of the USB drive. The recordings are saved in the Sounds folder inside this patch. The Organelle keyboard plays back the 24 most recent recordings.  Knob 1 controls input monitor level.
