## Details

Ypolimenti is a sample looping patch that combines the powers of the Segmenti and Pow Pow’s Polybeats patches!

After you record a sample, it is sliced into 24 even segments distributed across the Organelle’s keyboard. Ypolimenti uses the polyrhythmic sequencer from Pow Pow’s Polybeats to loop selected segments at different beat divisions of a single measure.

It is a multipage patch – the knobs change roles depending on page.
