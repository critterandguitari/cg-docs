Punch in a sequence of notes and quickly transpose it!  Make weird arpeggios and melodies.

## Details

Press and hold the Aux button to enter sequence record mode. Then 'punch' in a sequence up to 32 notes in length.  While punching the notes in, Knob1 controls the loudness of each note. Press Aux when you are finished.  Now the sequence will be transposed to whatever key you press.

In Play mode the knobs adjust tempo, pitch, tone and decay.  Hit the Aux button to latch your arpeggio and make a looping jam! The currently pressed or latched key will have a double wide circle on the OLED keyboard.  
  
Punchy sends and receives MIDI notes too, so you can set arpeggios with low notes with a MIDI keyboard and also send them to another synth!

Link enabled for syncing with other devices on a shared wireless network.

Play Mode (Default):

Knob1: Tempo

Knob2: Tuning

Knob3: Tone

Knob4: Decay Time

Aux: Quick Press: Latch. Long Press: Enter Punch Mode.

Foot Switch: N/A

Punch Mode:

Knob1: Note Velocity

Aux: Return to Play Mode

Foot Switch: N/A
