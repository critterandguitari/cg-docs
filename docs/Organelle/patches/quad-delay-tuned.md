## Details

This is very similar to the Quad Delay, except in this one the delay lines are much shorter and tuned to the keys.  Frequencies that resonate with the delay times get reinforced leading to some strange sounds.  It is especially fun shaping feedback (sticking the mic up to the speaker).  The Aux button and foot switch turn the effect on and off.
