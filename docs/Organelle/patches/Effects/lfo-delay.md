## Details

LFO Delay is an audio effect for the Organelle. Process incoming sounds with a plentitude of modulation.

There are two delay lines. Their delay times are controlled by independent LFOs. Set the maximum delay time for each line and let the LFO oscillate between that time and zero. Freeze an LFO by turning its rate down for a sustained delay time.

Use the sequencer to automate delay time ratio and feedback. The lower keyboard octave selects the ratio of max delay line times (C key is 1:1, B key is 1:6.5). The upper octave controls feedback (C key is 0%, B key is 99%).

Knob1: Delay A LFO Rate

Knob2: Delay B LFO Rate

Knob3: Maximum Delay Time for A & B (ratio is set by lower keyboard octave)

Knob4: Dry/Wet Mix

Aux and Foot Switch: Sequencer
