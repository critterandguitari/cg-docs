Play chords with one key and control how quickly notes of the chord are 'rolled.'

## Details

Chords Roll allows you to play entire chords by pressing one key.  You can select from 12 different chord types with Knob2, and then control how fast the chord is 'rolled.'  The notes comprising the chords are sent out over MIDI on Channel 2.

Knob1: Tuning

Knob2: Chord Type

Knob3: Roll Speed

Knob4: Decay Time

Aux: Sequencer Control & Waveform select. Use top row of keys to select options in the Menu. Lower row of keys selects the sequence record 'slot.'  
The 'Uni' option toggles on/off the ability to turn on more than one waveform at a time.
