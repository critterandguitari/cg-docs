## Details

Constant Gardener is a 'live' phase vocoder. Send it sound via the audio input jack and and you will be able to change its pitch and speed independently.

The keys control specific pitch intervals from an octave below original (low C) to a major 7th above original. Unison is the upper C key on the Organelle keyboard.

Knob1: Speed  
Knob2: Tuning  
Knob3: Dry / Wet Mix  
Knob4: Reverb  
Aux & Footswitch: Start & Stop phase vocoder.
