Polyphonic version of 'Nice Surprises.' Three square wave oscillators combine to increase levels of (dis)harmony. One oscillator produces 'junk'/random frequencies that adds nice surprises.

## Details

The same synth as Nice Surprises but with four-voice polyphony. Control three square wave oscillators with this patch. Keyboard controls the base frequency of one oscillator, also tunable with knob 2. The second oscillator plays a selectable harmonic above the base frequency. The third oscillator plays a adjustable amount of random junk frequencies at the beginning of each note.   Set the amount of 'Slidiness' as oscillators glide to their next frequency. Aux button controls a sequencer: hold it down to enable recording, begin playing to start a recording, and press Aux again to finish.

Knob1: Harmonic Selector

Knob2: Base Frequency

Knob3: 'Junk' Time

Knob4: Glide Amount

Aux: Sequencer Control (Use top row of keys to select options in the Menu)
