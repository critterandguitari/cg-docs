Record a sample and instantly get 24 variations of it! One 'clean' version and 23 'mutated' versions: one for each key! Resample your mutations to build up new ones! Endless!

## Details

Mutations 23 records a sample and creates 23 variations (mutations) of it. Each key of the keyboard controls one mutation: different speed and pitch relationships, reverb, filters, delay, phasor, and resorting parts of the sample.

The in/out points of the sample can be adjusted. Sequence the mutations.

The best part is that you resample one or more mutations in ‘Parenting’ mode for all kinds of fun. While you ‘parent,’ you can also record incoming sound to add to your sound’s journey!

Knob1: Mode (Record, Jam, Parenting)  
Knob2: Input Volume  
Knob3: Sample In Point  
Knob4: Sample Playback Length  
Aux: Depends on the mode. It either controls recording, sequencing, or parenting  
Foot Switch: Depends on the mode. It either controls recording, sequencing, or parenting

To save your sample, use the 'Save' or 'Save New' command in Storage menu.
