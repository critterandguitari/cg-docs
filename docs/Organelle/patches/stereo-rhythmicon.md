Stereo Rhythmicon is a playground of looped overtones. Based on the original instrument invented by Henry Cowell and Leon Theremin.

## Details

The Organelle keyboard is divided in half: the lower octave choses the overtones in the loop, and the upper octave transposes the result.

The mix is fed into a circular, stereo panner, swirling the tones around in space. The knobs control overall tone quality, tempo and tuning.

Knob1: Base Frequency

Knob2: Tempo

Knob3: Brightness

Knob4: Decay

Aux: Latch Cancel

Foot Switch: N/A

Link enabled for syncing with other devices on a shared wireless network.
