## Details

DJ Patch Record is a 'beat-juggling' sample looper. It places two independent playheads on one sample. Change the relative position and playback speed of the second playhead to the master playhead for phasing, echo, simultaneous forward & backward playback, and detuned effects. Master playhead is the thick line. Second playhead is thin line. A quick press on the Aux/Footswitch starts and stops playback. A long press will record enable a new track. There is a ten track maximum!

Knob1: Selects from 10 different samples

Knob2: Crossfade between playheads

Knob3: Sets the master playhead speed (-200% to +200%)

Knob4: Sets the second playhead speed (-200% to +200%)

Aux and Foot Switch: Playback Start/Stop & Record

The keys set the position of the second playhead in relation to the master. Customize the sounds by dropping .wav or .aiff files into the ‘sounds' folder and the patch will load them alphabetically and print the name to the screen.

To save newly recorded samples, use the 'Save' or 'Save New' command in Storage menu.
